Explain Binary Search algorithm

Include:
- Sorted array
- Middle element
- Divide search space
- Time complexity

ans:

    -Binary Search only works on a Sorted array. If the list is random, this method won't work.
    -Instead of checking every number one by one, we look at the Middle element of the array first.
        -If the middle matches our target, we are done.
        -If our target is larger, we ignore the left side.
        -If our target is smaller, we ignore the right side.
    -By checking the middle, we Divide the search space in half at every single step. We keep repeating this half-cut process until we find the target. 
    This makes the search fast.
    -the Time complexity is O(log n) bcoz we cut in half every time.
